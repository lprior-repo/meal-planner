# Draft and deploy

> **Context**: import DocCard from '@site/src/components/DocCard';

Draft, test, and deploy scripts, flows, and apps Windmill to iterate and save safely.

:::info Deploy to prod in Windmill

For all details on Deployments to prod in Windmill, see [Deploy to prod](./meta-12_deploy_to_prod-index.md).

:::

Each script, flow or app can be saved (sometimes concurrently) in 3 fashions, by your browser in `local storage`, by the backend as a `draft`, and `deployed`.

Once deployed, scripts, flows and apps will be visible, editable and/or runnable by users [with the right permissions](./meta-16_roles_and_permissions-index.md).
