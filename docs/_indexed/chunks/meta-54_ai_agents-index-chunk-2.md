## Configuration

### Provider selection

Choose from [supported AI providers](./meta-22_ai_generation-index.md#models) including OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, DeepSeek, Google AI (Gemini), Groq, OpenRouter, Together AI, or Custom AI endpoints.

### Resource configuration

Select or create an AI resource that contains your API credentials and endpoint configuration. [Resources](./meta-3_resources_and_types-index.md) allow you to securely store and reuse AI provider credentials across multiple flows.

### Model selection

Choose the specific model you want to use from your selected provider. Available models depend on your chosen provider and resource configuration.
