## Job debouncing in Script & Flows

### Job debouncing of a script

[Job debouncing of a script](./tutorial-script_editor-settings.md#debouncing) can be set from the [Settings](./tutorial-script_editor-settings.md) menu. Pick "Runtime" and then "Debouncing" and define a time window and optionally a custom debounce key.

### Job debouncing of a flow

From the Flow Settings Advanced menu, pick "Debouncing" and define a time window and optionally a custom debounce key.
