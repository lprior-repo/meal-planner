## How to do it

Within a [flow](./meta-6_flows_quickstart-index.md), pick the step **after which** you want to suspend:

- go to `Advanced`
- click on `Sleep`
- set the duration of the pause
- toggle on

The bed icon will show the sleep step is activated.
